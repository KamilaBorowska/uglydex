Overview

  • Mostly outclassed by other Fighting-types
  • Prankster Copycat sets it apart, allowing it to hit twice in a row most of the time, as well as revenge killing random threats.

Prankster

Riolu @ Eviolite
Ability: Prankster
EVs: 196 HP / 196 Def / 116 SpD
Impish Nature
- Copycat
- Protect
- Drain Punch
- Toxic / Crunch

Moves

  • Prankster Copycat allows it to copy the last used move with priority. This means it can usually hit Drain Punch twice, or revenge Pokemon such as Scraggy or Dwebble.
  • It can also be used to copycat things such as U-turn or Volt Switch from a teammate to keep up momentum, or set up things such as the opposing teams hazards or boosting moves.
  • Protect works amazingly well with Prankster as it allows it to protect itself from, and then use certain moves such as Foongus's Spore. -Drain Punch is the main STAB move, providing decent recovery, especially when Copycat allows it to use it twice in a row.
  • Toxic is great, and can be used, with priority, to wear down bulky foes.
  • Crunch is the best option against Ghost-type switch ins.

Set Details

  • Eviolite is the preferred item as it maximizes Riolu's bulk.
  • EVs are heavily invested in Physical Defense, which allows it to take on Physical attackers, such as Scraggy, with ease.
  • The rest is put into Special Defense to give it some Special Bulk.

Usage Tips

  • Riolu should be used as a pivot, switching into the Dark-, Rock-, Steel- and Normal-types it walls.
  • Often Copycat has very situational uses, so make sure you think about the plays you can make with it.

Team Options

  • VoltTurn users such as Chinchou can often be used to get Riolu in safely, and then Riolu can copycat their Volt Switch or U-Turn to preserve momentum.
  • Riolu needs something that can take on Poison-, Psychic-, Fairy-, or Flying-types. Cottonee and Fletchling are especially problematic as they can use faster priority.
  • Chinchou helps with taking on Flying-types.
  • Pawniard can switch into Cottonee, as well as Psychic-types moves.

Other Options

  • Riolu can boost its stats with Swords Dance, Agility or Bulk Up, but its boosting sets are generally outclassed by other Pokemon.
  • High Jump Kick is an option for a powerful STAB move and with Copycat, it can use it twice in a row to deal quite a lot of damage.
  • Iron Tail is its best option against Spritzee, but its low accuracy is off-putting.
